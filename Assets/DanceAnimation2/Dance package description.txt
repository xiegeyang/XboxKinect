Dance Package

This package features a collection of fbx animations for a bipedal humanoid character performing various dance moves

High Rythm Dance 01:A series of dance moves set to a quick tempo

High Rythm Dance 02:A series of rapid dance moves set to a quick tempo

High Rythm Dance 03:A series of dance moves with a great deal of movement and some breakdancing set to a quick tempo

High Rythm Dance 04:A series of rapid dance moves culminating in a pirouette 

Mid Rythm Dance 01:A series of dance moves set to a medium tempo

Mid Rythm Dance 02:An alternate series of dance moves set to a medium tempo

Mid Rythm Dance 03:A series of dance moves with rapid arm and hand movements set to a medium tempo

Slow Rythm Dance 01:A series of robotic dance moves set to a slow tempo

Slow Rythm Dance 02:a series of fluid dance moves set to a slow tempo

Moonwalk Dance: Moonwalking first backwards then in a sideways spin
